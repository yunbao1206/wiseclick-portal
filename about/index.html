About WiseClick

Our Mission

WiseClick provides free, accurate, and easy-to-use online tools that help people make smarter decisions — from finances and insurance to health and fitness. We believe everyone deserves access to the same quality of tools that professionals use, without any cost or barriers.

What We Offer

WiseClick is a growing network of free online tools. Our current sites include:

FinanceToolHub

12 free financial calculators covering mortgage, loans, compound interest, credit cards, auto loans, retirement, 401(k), taxes, annuities, savings, and paycheck estimation for all 50 US states.

InsureWise

8 free insurance calculators for auto, life, home, health, pet, travel, insurance needs assessment, and deductible vs premium comparison.

FitCalc

Health and fitness calculators to help you reach your wellness goals.

Our Principles

  • Free Forever — No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no paywalls.
  • Privacy First — All calculations run in your browser. We never collect or store your personal financial data.
  • Accuracy — We use the same formulas that banks and financial institutions use.
  • Accessibility — Our tools work on any device, anywhere, with no sign-up required.
